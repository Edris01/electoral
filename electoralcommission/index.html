About Electoral Commission

The electoral commission was set up under article 60 of the 1995 constitution of the republic of Uganda. it consists of the chairman, deputy chairman and five other members appointed by the president with the approval of parliment and secretariat headed by the secretary

details covering the commisions establishment and mandate can be accessedin articles 60 to 67 inclusive.

the functions of the EC are outlined under article 61 of the 1995 constitution of the republic of Uganda as follows;

  1. to ensure that regular, free and fair elections elections are held;
  2. to organise, conduct and supervise elections and referanda in accordance with the constitution;
